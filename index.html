Hi, I'm Wilhelm Willie.

But you can just call me Willie. I'm a freshman at the University of Southern California studying computer science and business administration.

At the moment, I am searching for an internship for the summer of 2017. Check out my projects, see what I do, and reach out to me for any questions, comments, or inquiries.
